What is a computer case fan?

A computer case fan is a fan that is installed into a computer case to create airflow to cool the components inside of the computer.

Why does motherboards so important?

Motherboard relay power to all computers within your PC and are responsible for communication between the components. Without the motherboard, your PC cannot function. They are also responsible for a lot of the base features your computer will come with, like the amount of RAM, Bluetooth or Wi-Fi speed.

Why CPU is important?

The CPU is the brain of a computer, containing all the circuitry needed to process input, store date, and output results. The CPU is constantly following instruction of computer programs that tell it which data to process and how to process. Without a CPU, we could not run programs on a computer.

What is the use of graphics?

Graphics cards are used in games and 3D-accelerated application. Graphics cards can be found in some desktop and laptop PCs. A graphic card can either be a dedicated card that is attached to the motherboard via an expansion slot, or they are embedded into the CPU or onto the system’s main circuit board. System like mobile phones, workstation, and game consoles will use either a dedicated or embedded card to display graphics.

Where is RAM located?

RAM is located on the motherboard of the computer adjacent to the CPU socket in DIMM slots that are installed side-by-side. Most users don’t need more than 4 slots, but motherboards can have 2, 4, 6, 8, or even 16 DIMM slots.

What does sound card do?

A sound card is a computer component that translates digital audio signals to analog ones, and analog audio signals to digital ones. Technically, a sound card is an optional component but a necessary one if you want to hear a sound from you PC on wired headphone or speakers.

What is the most important thing in Hard disk drive?

The most important characteristic of a hard disk drive is storage capacity. The primary function of a hard drive is to store files, and the storage capacity defines how much can be stored on a single drive. A typical hard drive for a new computer has a storage capacity of 500 GB.

What is the main purpose of a monitor?

A computer monitor is an output device that displays information in pictorials or textual form. A discrete monitor comprises a visual display, support electronics, power supply, housing, electrical connectors, and external user controls.

What is the purpose of a USB Flash drive?

A USB drive, also referred to as a flash drive or memory stick, is a small portable device that plugs into the USB port on your computer. USB drives are commonly used for storage data backup, and transferring files between devices.
