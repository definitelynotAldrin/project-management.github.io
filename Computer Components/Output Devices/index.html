Monitor

A monitor is an electronic display device that displays images or information from a computer or other source. It allows users to view digital content, such as documents, photos, videos, and websites.

Headset

A headset is a combination of headphones and a microphone, often used for communication purposes in video or audio conferences, gaming, or listening to music privately.

Speaker

A speaker is a device that converts electrical signals into audible sound waves. It is commonly used for listening to music, watching videos, or participating in audio conferences.

Printer

A printer is a peripheral device that creates a physical copy of an electronic document or image. It does this by transferring ink, toner, or other materials onto paper or other media.

Video Projector

A video projector is a device that projects video and images onto a screen or surface. It is commonly used for presentations, movies, and other large-scale visual displays.
