Image Scanner

An image scanner is a device that is used to scan printed or handwritten documents, photos, or other images, and convert them into digital files. It typically uses a combination of light sensors and software to create a digital copy of the original image.

Keyboard

A keyboard is a computer peripheral that is used for inputting text and commands into a computer system. It typically contains a set of keys that correspond to letters, numbers, and symbols, as well as additional keys for special functions and shortcuts.

Mouse

A mouse is a computer peripheral that is used for pointing, clicking, and scrolling on a graphical user interface (GUI). It typically contains one or more buttons and a scroll wheel, and may also have additional features such as a touchpad or trackball.

Web Camera

A web camera, also known as a webcam, is a digital camera that is used to capture live video and transmit it over the internet. It is commonly used for video conferencing, online streaming, and video chatting.

Microphone

A microphone is a device that converts sound waves into an electrical signal. It is commonly used for recording audio, as well as for voice communication in telephony and computer applications.
